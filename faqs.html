  • Daniel, how much does a website cost?
    I understand your question about cost, and it's a valid question. It's a bit like ordering a custom suit. Imagine you are looking for a unique suit or dress. The price will depend on several factors, such as the type of fabric, design, personalized details and even the designer you choose.

    Likewise, when creating a website, the cost depends on your specific needs. Customized features, exceptional design, and advanced functionality can influence the budget. But don't worry, I'm here to help you set a budget that perfectly meets your requirements. As soon as we have the details, I will be available to explain exactly what you can expect from your investment.
  • Daniel, how long does it take to create a website?
    I understand your question. In fact, the time required to complete a website project can vary depending on the requirements and scope. Generally, smaller projects tend to take around 2 months from start to launch. These projects tend to be more direct, with less complex features and pages.

    However, when we talk about larger projects, which involve more advanced features, custom design and a broader scope, the process can take 3 to 4 months. This is because there are more elements to be developed, tested and refined.

    Keep in mind that these are general deadlines and may vary depending on the complexity of the project and the collaboration between the development team and the client. Effective communication and clear definition of requirements are essential to ensure the project is delivered on time and meets expectations.
