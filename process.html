Process

  1. 1.
    Briefing
    I would like to receive a comprehensive document with the following information: an introduction to you and your brand identity, your core brand principles, the goals you aim for your business, who your target audience is, details about your products or services, what resources are needed, what are the limits of the project (or a website outline), the deadlines and relevant data, what reference sites you admire, and any other information that you consider relevant to your idea of a website.
  2. 2.
    Evaluation
    Your brief will be carefully scrutinized to ensure the project aligns with both parties' expectations and requirements. If we find a balance between your needs and our capabilities, we will be happy to schedule an online meeting to discuss next steps and how we can best move forward.
  3. 3.
    Initiation
    We will begin the journey by working together to explore different approaches and concepts, with the aim of finding the solution that best meets your needs. We are open to holding multiple discussion sessions if necessary to ensure we reach the perfect solution.
  4. 4.
    Development
    After approval of the design and concept and references, we will begin the development of the corresponding screens. I will keep you regularly informed about development progress, so you have full visibility of progress throughout the process.
  5. 5.
    Quality
    After we complete the development phase, I will perform extensive testing across multiple browsers and devices to identify and resolve potential issues. Only after receiving your final approval will we consider the site ready to be launched and made available.
